# Joy Ram Steel - Enterprise POS Documentation

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the logical engines and features powering the Joy Ram Steel (JRS) Point of Sale and Inventory Management System.

---

## 🏗️ Core Architecture
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router) & React 19.
- **Local Database:** Dexie.js (IndexedDB) for high-performance offline storage.
- **Cloud Sync:** Supabase (Postgres) for bi-directional data persistence.
- **Media Storage:** Cloudinary (Primary) with Supabase Storage (Fallback).
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS 4 & Framer Motion for enterprise-grade UI/UX.

---

## ⚡ Logical Engines

### 1. The Sync & Conflict Engine (CRDT)
- **Logic:** Bi-directional synchronization every 10 seconds.
- **Conflict Resolution:** Uses a `version_clock` (Logical Clock). The highest version always wins during a sync.
- **Deletion Integrity:** Implements a "Hard-Purge" pull. If an item is marked `is_deleted: 1` in the cloud, it is instantly removed from local storage during the next sync to prevent "ghost data."

### 2. Perfect Search & Barcode Engine
- **Fuzzy Search:** Powered by the **Levenshtein Distance** algorithm. It corrects typos (e.g., "thla" → "thal") in real-time.
- **Optical Recognition:**
  - **Auto-Detect:** Instantly adds items to the bill if a barcode is scanned or typed (Zero-Click).
  - **Camera Scanner:** Integrated high-speed mobile camera scanning via `html5-qrcode`.
  - **Image Upload:** Recognition of barcodes directly from uploaded photos/screenshots.
- **Auto-Generator:** Automatically creates unique `890` prefix barcodes for every new variant or bulk import.

### 3. Pricing & Combo Engine
- **Pricing Modes:**
  - **Standard:** Price per single unit.
  - **Combo Deal:** Pack-based pricing (e.g., 4 units for total ₹100).
  - **Weight:** Billed by KG (decimal support).
- **Auto-Unit Mapping:** Selling a "Pack of 4" automatically handles the math to deduct exactly 4 single units from physical stock.

### 4. Media & Compression Engine
- **One-Touch Compression:** Uses `browser-image-compression` to ensure all product photos are optimized to **<300KB** before they touch the network.
- **Dual-Path Upload:**
  - Primary: Uploads directly to **Cloudinary**.
  - Fallback: If Cloudinary is unreachable, it silently uploads to **Supabase Storage**.

### 5. Document & PDF Engine
- **Branded Invoices:** Client-side generation of high-fidelity GST and eWay bills.
- **Khata Ledger:** Generates professional A4 PDF Ledger Statements with brand logos and transaction tables.
- **Zero-Storage Logic:** All documents are generated on-the-fly from raw data, meaning ₹0 storage costs for your Supabase plan.

## 📦 Core Modules

### 🛒 POS Terminal
- **Park & Resume:** Ability to save a current cart and start a new one (Multi-cart support).
- **Bargain Console:** Interactive slider to apply discounts while monitoring **MSP (Minimum Support Price)** safety limits.
- **Duplicate Shield:** Prevents double-billing during network lag.
- **Keyboard Mastery:** Entirely operable via `Alt + P` (Pay), `Alt + C` (Clear), and `Cmd + K` (Search).

### 📋 Inventory Dashboard
- **Master Brands:** Unique management of brand headers (e.g., MILTON) vs. Variants (e.g., 5L, 10L).
- **Valuation Radar:** Real-time calculation of Total Asset Worth based on cost price vs. retail price.
- **Stock Health:** Distribution alerts for critical and healthy stock levels.

### 📘 Digital Khata
- **Market Credit:** Live tracking of "Udhar" per customer.
- **WhatsApp Handoff:** Pre-filled professional payment reminders sent directly to the customer's phone.

### 🛡️ GST Vault
- **Digital Archive:** Automatically stores every invoice generated by the system.
- **Bill Scanner:** Digitizes physical supplier invoices via the mobile camera for permanent records.

---

## 🌍 Localization & Accessibility
- **Translation Engine:** Instant toggle between **English** and **Bengali** for the entire interface.
- **Admin Manual:** An interactive bilingual manual built into the header notification system.
- **PWA Ready:** Installable as a native app on Android/iOS; works perfectly in low-connectivity areas.
